Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.
You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below,
but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Eggs
  • Cheese

Step-by-step

    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Parmesan cheese
  2. Repeat until you're satisfied
