Grilled cheese

Homepage

Description

Everyone needs to know how to make a classic grilled cheese sandwich.
Whether you're a beginner cook or an old pro,
you'll come back to this top-rated grilled cheese recipe again and again.

Ingredients

  • White sliced bread
  • Butter
  • Cheddar cheese

Step by step

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
    Generously butter one side of a slice of bread.
    Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese.
    Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  2. Cook until lightly browned on one side;
    flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
